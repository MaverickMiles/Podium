Welcome to Podium, a platform designed for discussion.

If this is your first time visiting, or would like to review Podium's complete community guidelines, please visit our help page.

Posts should be
Relevant and designed to persuade other users and to open up civil debate.
Grammatically clean and follow a cohesive argument.

The following are highly discouraged
Low effort or off-topic posts
Purposefully inflammatory posts or targeted insults

The following will be deleted by moderators
Aggressive, provocative language targeted at an individual or group.
Advertising or the selling of anything of any kind.
Depictions of extremely graphic violence or explicit content.
Posts containing anything considered illegal by the United States government.
